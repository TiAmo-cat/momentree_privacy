Momentree

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

Momentree helps users track urges, recovery progress, and habit consistency.

What data the app stores

Momentree stores progress data locally on the user's device, including streak count, momentum points, craving resistance count, relapse count, recovery usage, and check-in history.

What data is not required

Momentree does not require account registration, email login, or cloud sync in order to use the core app.

Advertising

Momentree uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob and its partners may collect or process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from network information, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and fraud-prevention signals.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Ads Technology: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Analytics

Momentree does not currently use a separate analytics SDK.

Data sharing

The app itself does not sell personal data. Advertising-related data may be processed by Google AdMob for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and policy enforcement.

Children

Momentree is not designed for children under 13.

Data retention

Local progress data remains on the device unless the user clears app data or uninstalls the app.
